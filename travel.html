Getting to

Newfoundland

Since Newfoundland is an island, we recommend traveling by air. The main and closest airport is the St. John's International Airport (YYT) which is a short 20 minute drive from the downtown area.

Getting Around

The best way to explore St. John's and the surrounding sights is by car. Rental cars are very limited in St. John's and so we recommend securing your rental as soon as possible.

Taxis are available throughout the city and there is public transit, the metro bus, however it is quite infrequent. There is also a hop-on-hop-off bus that travels to popular attractions.

Places to Stay

The downtown St. John's area is the heart of the city. Filled with local shops, tasty coastal food and salty ocean air as it sits just above the harbour. It also happens to be where most of our wedding events take place so we recommend staying within the area. There are lots of places to stay ranging from well-known hotel chains, local boutique hotels and BnB's.

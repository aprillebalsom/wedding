Getting There

Flatrock is about a 30 minute drive from downtown St. John's. There will be a shuttle bus available from the downtown area for transportation to and from the ceremony & toast in Flatrock. For those who prefer to drive, there is limited parking available on the property and along the road.

The boat excursion and after party are both located in the downtown area. Taxis as well as paid street parking are available.

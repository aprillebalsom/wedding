We're Going Unplugged

We invite you to be fully present with us during our ceremony. We kindly ask that you switch off all phones & cameras as we wish to keep our ceremony an intimate event.

Transportation

Our ceremony & toast venue is in Flatrock which is about a 30 minute drive from downtown St. John's. There will be a shuttle bus available from the downtown area for transportation to and from the venue in Flatrock.

  • Downtown Pickup/Drop off Location: In front of the Delta Hotel Entrance on New Gower Street! The closest main intersection is Barter's Hill & New Gower Street. Look for the bus parking sign and a white school bus.
  • Pickup time: Please be at the downtown pickup spot for 3pm. The bus will be leaving shortly after this. The bus will return to this same drop off location around 6pm as the ceremony and toast is finished at 5:30pm.

For those who prefer to drive, there is limited parking available on the property and along the road.

The boat excursion and after party are both located in the downtown area. Taxis as well as paid street parking are available.
